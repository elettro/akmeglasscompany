About Akme Glass

Let the Light In.
Keep the Noise Out.

The main function of any fence is to provide a barrier — but wood or wrought iron fences can become an eyesore. Glass lets the light in, keeps out noise and wind, and creates an unobtrusive construction that lets you see through the fence, not the fence itself.

  • Tempered glass panels in a variety of thicknesses for durability and safety
  • All metals specially coated for superior resistance to harsh environments
  • Galvanized-steel stiffeners resist concrete chemicals that destroy aluminum
  • Minimal maintenance compared to wood or wrought iron alternatives
  • All glass fencing guaranteed free from material and workmanship defects

Engineering & System Design

A Better Design.
Built to Last.

The way a fence is anchored is everything. Akme Glass uses an engineered system that outperforms competitors — and stands up to the harshest environments.

Galvanized-Steel Stiffeners

Unlike competitors who sink aluminum directly into concrete — causing deterioration over time — we use galvanized-steel stiffeners that resist concrete chemicals for a lifetime of performance.

Kynar-Finished Aluminum Framework

Baked-on kynar finish on all aluminum extrusions is more durable than powder-coated paint. The framework features a precision pocket that tempered glass panels are inserted into.

Vinyl Gasket Weather Seal

A vinyl gasket inserted between the aluminum framework and glass panel resists weather and vibration, holding the panel firmly in place through years of exposure.
